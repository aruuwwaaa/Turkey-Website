What is interesting about Turkey?

Turkey is not only a magnificent coast, picturesque beaches and charming gentle sea. In addition, it is a country whose small territory can easily accommodate the richest heritage of different cultures and civilizations, their wisdom and beauty.

Amazing nature, golden sandy beaches, picturesque mountains covered with relict pine forests, the bright sun and, of course, crystal clear, gentle and warm sea - all this makes resorts in Turkey attractive to tourists. Turkey is a country of living culture, a country of ancient civilizations.
